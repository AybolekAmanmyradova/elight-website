Our Purpose

What drives us.

Mission

To equip youth across Central Asia with the English skills, academic confidence, and career readiness they need to compete and lead in a global world. We do this through modern, technology-powered education that meets students where they are.

Vision

To become Turkmenistan's most trusted and results-driven online English school, where every young person who comes to us, online or in person, leaves better prepared for the future they deserve.

FAQ

Common questions.

How do I sign up? ＋

Fill out the form and a member of our team will reach out to you within 24 hours. Do not forget to add a WhatsApp or Telegram phone number, or handle for your socials so we can contact you. Or message us on Instagram, TikTok, WhatsApp or Telegram at +1 (925) 834-0107.

I don't know my English language level. Can you help? ＋

Yes! We will send you a test to determine your English language level.

Do you accept payments from my country? ＋

Yes, we accept payments from everywhere. We accept cash payments in Ashgabat, Sberbank in Russia, Zelle in the USA, and PayPal and card payments from everywhere else. Message us and we will help you with the payment.

Can I choose my lesson times? ＋

For individual lessons, we will schedule lesson times based on your availability. For group lessons, we will do our best to find a time that works for the majority.

Can I meet the instructors? ＋

Please check our Instagram page to learn about our instructors.
